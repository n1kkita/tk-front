TaskManager is a project aimed at managing tasks and organizing work processes. This system allows you to effectively organize tasks for yourself, your team, or employees, track their performance, and plan your workflow.

Features:

  • Create tasks and assign them to individuals or groups.
  • Set deadlines and priorities for tasks.
  • Track the progress of tasks and receive notifications.
  • Collaborate with team members in real-time.
  • Generate reports and analytics on task performance.

Get started today and streamline your workflow with Task Manager!

For more details, check out the Documentation or manage your Groups.
